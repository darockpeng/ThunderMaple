Our Story

Built for
the way
offices eat.

"Fresh sushi for modern workplaces — because great food should be easy, not complicated."

Thunder Maple was created to bring fresh, well-crafted Japanese-inspired food to the everyday places where people work, meet, and gather — right here in Thunder Bay.

Built on hands-on experience in sushi operations and food production, we focus on what matters most to business customers: freshness, consistency, efficiency, and reliable service. We understand that great catering is not only about taste, but also about timing, presentation, and making group ordering simple.

Mission

To make workplace meals fresh, reliable, and easy to order.

Vision

To become Thunder Bay's most trusted Japanese-inspired catering brand for offices and community events.
